# Life Simulator — A Financial Planning Game

Turn your future into a game. Play your life. Learn your money.

Life Simulator is an interactive, story-driven financial planning experience that transforms post-graduation life decisions into a dynamic simulation. Instead of static calculators, this tool immerses users in a narrative where every choice—career, education, housing, debt, and lifestyle—shapes their long-term financial future.

---

## Overview

What happens after graduation?

- Should you go to college—or skip it?
- Pay off debt aggressively or invest early?
- Live cheaply or enjoy your income now?
- Take risks or play it safe?

In Life Simulator, you do not just calculate outcomes—you experience them.

Progress year-by-year through adulthood, encounter realistic life events, and watch your financial trajectory unfold in real time.

---

## Key Features

### Story-Driven Gameplay
- Experience AI-generated life events tailored to your character  
- Navigate promotions, setbacks, emergencies, and opportunities  
- Every decision has both immediate and long-term consequences  

### Post-Graduation Paths
Choose how your life begins:

- 4-Year University  
- Community College → Transfer  
- Trade School  
- Workforce Entry  
- Military Service  

Each path affects:

- Debt  
- Income potential  
- Career opportunities  

---

### Career and Income Simulation
- Select careers with different:
  - Salaries  
  - Growth rates  
  - Stress levels  

Career paths include:

- Big Tech  
- Healthcare  
- Startups  
- Skilled Trades  
- Sales and commission roles  

---

### Real-Life Financial Decisions
Make meaningful choices around:

- Housing (rent, buy, live at home)  
- Debt strategies (minimum, aggressive, forgiveness)  
- Lifestyle (spend vs invest)  
- Side hustles and additional income  

---

### Dynamic Life Events
Each year introduces:

- Unexpected expenses (medical bills, car repairs)  
- Windfalls (bonuses, raises)  
- Economic shifts (recessions, job changes)  

These events:

- Affect income, savings, and debt  
- Force trade-offs under uncertainty  

---

### Visual Feedback and Analytics
- Track your:
  - Net worth  
  - Savings rate  
  - Debt levels  
- View charts of financial growth over time  
- Review a timeline of your life decisions  

---

### Beat the AI Mode
- Compete against an AI financial planner  
- Same starting conditions and events  
- Compare outcomes to see who builds more wealth  

---

### Leaderboard
- Save and compare high scores  
- Compete for the best financial outcome  
- Replay to optimize your strategy  

---

## Learning Goals

This project helps users understand:

- The power of compounding  
- Trade-offs between debt and investing  
- How early decisions shape long-term outcomes  
- The impact of risk, uncertainty, and lifestyle inflation  
- Why financial planning is dynamic, not static

## How to Play

1. Choose a game mode:
   - Standard  
   - Random Life  
   - Beat the AI  

2. Create your character:
   - Select your background  
   - Set your starting conditions  

3. Make key life decisions:
   - Education path  
   - Career choice  
   - Housing situation  
   - Debt strategy  

4. Progress through life year by year:
   - Respond to events and scenarios  
   - Adapt your financial strategy  

5. Reach the end of your timeline:
   - Review your financial results  
   - Compare your performance
